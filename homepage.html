Signs of Suicide

Withdrawal from friends and family, loss of interest in activities, extreme mood swings, and talking about feeling hopeless or wanting to die are key warning signs.

How You Can Help

Talk openly, listen without judgment, and encourage them to seek professional help. Your support can make a difference.
